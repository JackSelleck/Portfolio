I am Jack Selleck, I am currently on the second year of my game development degree and have wanted to make games since i was a child. I have already made a variety of games ranging from my action game "Krungus", my procedurally generated rhythm game, and my in progress RPG "Deeper"; as well as a shooter and story game, which were made too early in my game development career to feature on my Portfolio. My primary aspiration in my game development career is to be a gameplay engineer, but i also like pixel art.

As of now, I am at the top of my class and always put all my effort towards my assignments. When I make a game I am always sure to finish it, and bring it to a standard which reflects my current skill level.
When I am not making games, I am often playing them, or spending time with friends. My favorite games to play are games where i can play against freinds, Story games, Rhythm games, Exploration games and Competitive games. I also like to learn and research new things, right now my main focus is getting better at C++, as it seems like a good challenge and a logical next step in my game development career.
